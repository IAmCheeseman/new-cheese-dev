A Rogue-like Metroidvania.

Red Nivis

Red Nivis is a rogue-like metroidvania where you explore the ruins of the Red Nivis Laboratory. Every run, you'll have to relearn the layout of the world, and you will be able to start with a set amount of abilities, just so long as you have that option unlocked.

  • ● 6 Unique Areas

    You have main areas to explore, each area has it's own identity and won't look, or function like another area.

  • ● 5 Unique Abilities

    Each ability opens up new options in the moment-to-moment gameplay.

  • ● 7 Challenging Bosses

    7 bosses are spread throughout the map for you to find, and only one is mandatory to beat the game. They are challenging and won't die very easily.

  • ● Tons of Crazy Weapons

    There are tons of weapons for you to choose from. You can even pick exactly which weapon you want with the weapon swapping system.

  • ● A Cast of Awesome NPCs

    You have a bunch of unique, and functional NPCs to talk to. Every NPC has it's use, no NPC is filler.

  • ● Great Worlds to Explore

    You'll always have a fun world to play in that captures the feeling that metroidvanias give, while also being in your own unique world. You have a ton of NPCs, weapons, and bosses to find, so you won't get bored any time soon.
