Red Nivis' Abilities

back

  • 1. Teleport

    Basically a dash. Has I-frames, so you can get out of sticky situations easier. Rewarded by H.A.N.K.

  • 2. Boots

    Allows you to do damage to enemies by stomping on their heads. (Previously a double jump.) Rewarded by the Gnome (with a stick.)

  • 3. Grenade

    Lets you throw a grenade. Rewarded by Billy.

  • 4. Hookshot

    You can throw out a hook, which pulls you to the hit point. If you hit an enemy with it, it pulls the enemy to you. Rewarded by The Muscular Fridge.

  • 5. Sticky Glove

    Allows you to stick to walls, and wall jump. Rewarded by The Rotten Burger.
