Red Nivis

back

Red Nivis is a rogue-like metroidvania where you explore the ruins of the Red Nivis Laboratory. Every run, you'll have to relearn the layout of the world, and you will be able to start with a set amount of abilities, just so long as you have that option unlocked.

Features

  • Multiple Unique Areas

    You have multiple main areas to explore, each area has it's own identity and won't look, or function like another area.

  • Challenging Bosses

    Bosses are spread throughout the map for you to find. They are challenging and won't die very easily.

  • Loads of Crazy Guns

    There are tons of weapons for you to choose from. You can even pick exactly which weapon you want with the weapon swapping system.

  • A Cast of Great NPCs

    You have a bunch of unique, and functional NPCs to talk to. Every NPC has it's use, no NPC is filler.

  • Great Worlds to Explore

    You'll always have a fun world to play in that captures the feeling that metroidvanias give while in a unique world.
