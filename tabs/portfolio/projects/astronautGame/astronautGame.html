Astronaut Game

My current, in development game. At the end of this article, there will be links to videos showing gameplay. But first, a description.

It's a roguelike metroidvania, sounds bad at first, but I have systems to help with progress, after you unlock an ability, you can unlock it permenantly, after doing a challenge of some sort. When you start a run, you can choose a couple abilities to start with. The rest you will have to unlock.

Goals

I want at least 6 biomes, with 3-4 weapons in each. The final game will have multiple enemies to find, and NPCs. There will be 6 bosses, and 5 abilities.
